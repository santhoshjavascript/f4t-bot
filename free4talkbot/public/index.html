Interactive Command Reference
!play [title]
Play music from YouTube by search query or URL.
!search [query]
Search top 10 songs on YouTube to select and play.
!skip / !s
Skip the currently playing song to the next in queue.
!stop
Stop audio playback and clear the playlist queue completely.
!queue / !q
View the list of scheduled songs in the music queue.
!np
Show details and a progress bar of the currently playing song.
!repeat / !r
Toggle song repeat mode ON or OFF for continuous loop.
!vol [1-100/max/min]
Adjust the bot playback volume dynamically.
!talkmode [on/off]
Toggle real-time voice wake-word listening on or off.
!voice
Get current voice synthesis, listening status, and keys.
!voiceai [on/off]
Let bot speak AI replies out loud over mic instead of text.
!voiceai [guy/aria]
Switch between Male (guy) and Female (aria) US English.
!voiceai pitch [baby/giant]
Change pitch to funny high-pitched or deep scary styles.
!ai [on/off/sleep]
Mute or unmute casual/random AI chat interjections.
!quiet / !unmute
Quickly quiet/silence the AI, or unmute it back.
!bass [0-20]
Apply low-frequency gain filters to boost bass.
!treble [0-20]
Apply high-frequency gain filters to boost treble.
!reverb
Toggle the virtual convolver/reverberation effect.
!8d
Toggle 8D rotating panning surrounding sound effect.
!nightcore
Toggle high speed and pitch nightcore music mode.
!slowed
Toggle chopped and slowed chill reverb sound effect.
!fxreset
Clear all applied audio filters and reset to default.
!daily
Claim your daily allowance of free economy coins.
!balance / !bal
Check your wallet and bank statement balances.
!leaderboard / !lb
Show the richest active room members in the community.
!slot [amount]
Place your bet on a triple multiplier slot machine.
!flip [heads/tails]
Double your wager in a simple coin flip mini-game.
!gacha
Spend coins to pull and roll items from the gacha pool.
!hunt
Embark on a local hunt for items, XP, and gold.
!adventure
Venture into hazardous dungeons for premium loot.
!shop
Browse goods, custom weapons, and status boosts for sale.
!inv / !inventory
Check your collected weapons, potions, and equipment.
!mine / !fish / !chop
Run specific gathering activities to gain materials.
